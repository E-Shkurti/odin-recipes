Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper. It is creamy without using cream and very quick to prepare.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Eggs
  • Pancetta
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Boil the spaghetti.
  2. Cook pancetta in a pan.
  3. Mix eggs and cheese in a bowl.
  4. Combine everything together.
  5. Serve hot with pepper.
