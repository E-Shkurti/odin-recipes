Pancakes

Description

Pancakes are soft, fluffy breakfast treats served with honey syrup.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Honey
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Mix all the ingredients together.
  2. Heat a pan over medium heat.
  3. Pour butter into the pan and cook on both sides until golden.
  4. Place the pancakes on a plate and drizzle honey on top.
