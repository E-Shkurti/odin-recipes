Pizza Margherita

Description

Pizza Margherita is a simple and delicious Italian pizza with tomato sauce, mozzarella, and fresh basil.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella
  • Fresh basil

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough.
  2. Add tomato sauce.
  3. Add cheese and basil.
  4. Bake in oven.
